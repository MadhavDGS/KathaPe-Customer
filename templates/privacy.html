Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

KathaPe Customer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you register and use our app:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password
  • Transaction Data: Credit amounts, payment amounts, transaction dates, and notes
  • Bill Images: Photos of bills/receipts you upload for transaction records
  • Business Information: Information about businesses you transact with

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Usage Data: App features used, pages visited, time spent on app
  • Location Data: We do NOT collect precise location data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our credit management services
  • Process and record your transactions
  • Store and display bill images associated with transactions
  • Calculate and display account balances with businesses
  • Send transaction notifications and updates
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Data Storage

  • Database: Appwrite Cloud (secure cloud database)
  • Images: Cloudinary (professional image hosting service)
  • Location: Data stored in secure cloud servers

3.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption (SSL/TLS)
  • Passwords hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Images stored with secure access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT sell your personal information.

4.2 We may share information with:

  • Business Partners: Businesses you transact with can see your name and transaction details
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate (Appwrite, Cloudinary, Render)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4.3 What We Share with Businesses:

When you transact with a business using KathaPe:

  • Your name and customer ID
  • Transaction amounts and dates
  • Bill images you upload
  • Current balance with that business

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your transaction history and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional communications

5.2 To exercise these rights:

Contact us at: support@khatape.tech

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as:

  • Your account remains active
  • Needed to provide you services
  • Required by law or regulations
  • Necessary for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where required to retain for legal or regulatory purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

KathaPe Customer is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Appwrite: Database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudinary: Image storage and delivery (Privacy Policy)
  • Render: Application hosting (Privacy Policy)

These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and similar tracking technologies:

  • Session Cookies: To keep you logged in
  • Functional Cookies: To remember your preferences
  • Analytics: To understand how you use the app (anonymized)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@khatape.tech
  • Website: https://khatape.tech

13. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable data protection laws including:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
  • Personal Data Protection Bill (India)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable
